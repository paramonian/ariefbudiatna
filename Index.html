Qualification Rules — Always Apply
Before Any Quote "Do you have an allocated budget confirmed for this project, or are we building the case for approval with your team?"
Before Any Brainstorm Brainstorm and creative direction sessions only happen after down payment is received. State this in every proposal.
Agency Red Flags New account · No common groups · Two contacts reaching from same client · Budget approval needed · Asking for production breakdown before committing · Going silent after receiving proposal detail.
Real Buyer Signals Confirms budget upfront · Asks for shoot date · Asks about deposit · References timeline with urgency · Decision maker is on the thread directly.
